UAS Equipment Considerations

January 2021 - May 2021

Capstone Project

New and emerging technology has always been an interest to me so when I got this opportunity I was really excited to work with everyone. The project was an analysis and proposal to the Albany Fire department on the use of Unmanned Aircraft System (UAS Equipment or Drones) for various missions. The paper goes in depth to various cost analysis, safety protocols, flying laws and regulations, and plenty more. For this project I had the honor to work with a fantastic hard working team that was able to keep track of group meetings, focus on research, handling deadlines and maintaining communication throughout the project.

I am very interested in technology that is just becoming mainstream. Tech like Drones, 3D printing, Virtual and augmented reality, and more, are all on the cutting edge and i'm very excited to see where this technology goes in the coming years.
